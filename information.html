Information

I am a Media Composer based in Brighton, United Kingdom.

I was very fortunate to have been offered lessons for multiple instruments when I was very young. These foundations on the Saxophone, Piano, Percussion and singing have each influenced my latter musical education and interests. In addition to instrument lessons, I gained a GCSE in Music, an A-level in Music Technology and music theory certificates up to and including Grade 7 from Christ's Hospital School in Horsham, West Sussex (UK).

Since leaving formal education, I have sought careers that involve music, including running choirs and musical productions in schools, to providing musical activities for children and young adults with additional emotional needs and learning disabilities.

Creating music has always been an interest of mine. Throughout my adult life, I have loved engaging in the cycle of making music followed by learning from that experience and starting over again. Picking up and learning new instruments is one of my most fulfilling experiences. I enjoy writing for a wide range of musical styles from small chamber pieces to larger orchestral tracks through to electronic styles. The music on Reaching the Water's Edge is the best representation of the style that I most enjoy and feel the most connected to emotionally.
